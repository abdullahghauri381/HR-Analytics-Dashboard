 HR Analytics Dashboard

  Project Overview

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in **Power BI** using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset. The dashboard helps analyze employee attrition by different factors such as department, age group, gender, education field, salary slab, and job role.

 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees
- Current Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate



 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

- Employees Left by Age Group
- Employees Left by Department
- Employees Left by Gender
- Employees Left by Education Field
- Employees Left by Salary Slab
- Employees Left by Job Role



 🔍 Key Insights

- Overall Attrition Rate: **16.1%**
- Research & Development has the highest employee attrition.
- Employees aged **26–35** show the highest attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest number of employees leaving.
- Most employees who left belong to the lower salary slabs.
